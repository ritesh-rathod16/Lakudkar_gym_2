Premium Diet Plan

📊 Daily Macros (Indian Foods)

💪 Protein: 1.6-2.2g/kg (Eggs 🥚, Chicken 🍗, Paneer 🧀, Dal 🌱)

⚡ Carbs: 3-5g/kg (Brown Rice 🍚, Roti 🫓, Oats 🥣)

🫒 Fats: 0.8-1g/kg (Ghee 🧈, Nuts 🥜, Coconut 🥥)

💧 Hydration: 3-4L water + Nimbu Pani 🍋 or Coconut Water 🥥

6-Day Gym Workout Split

Goal: Build muscle 💪, strength 🔥 & endurance 🏃‍♂️

Rest Day: Sunday 🧘 (active recovery: yoga/walking)

Day 1 (Monday): Chest & Triceps

  • Flat Bench Press – 4x6-8 🏋️‍♂️
  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 3x8-10 🏋️‍♀️
  • Cable Flys – 3x12 ✨
  • Triceps Dips – 3xMAX 💪
  • Skull Crushers – 3x10-12 🦾
  • (Finish: 10 min incline treadmill walk 🚶‍♂️)

Day 2 (Tuesday): Back & Biceps

  • Pull-Ups – 4x6-8 (add weight if advanced) 🏋️‍♂️
  • Barbell Rows – 3x8-10 🏋️‍♀️
  • Lat Pulldown – 3x10-12 🔄
  • Deadlifts – 3x5 (heavy!) ⚡
  • Hammer Curls – 3x12 💥
  • (Stretch: 5 min lats/biceps 🧘)

6-Day Calisthenics Plan

Goal: Build strength 💪, muscle 🏋️‍♂️, and mobility 🧘‍♂️

Rest Day: Sunday (active recovery: yoga/stretching) 🧘‍♀️

Day 1 (Monday): Upper Body Push

  • Push-Ups – 4x12-15 🏋️‍♂️ (slow, full range)
  • Pike Push-Ups – 3x8-10 ✋ (shoulder focus)
  • Dips (Parallel Bars/Chair) – 3x8-10 🔄
  • Handstand Holds – 3x20-30 sec ⏳ (against wall)
  • Triceps Extensions – 3x10-12 🦾
  • (Finish: 5 min wrist mobility drills ✋)

Day 2 (Tuesday): Upper Body Pull

  • Pull-Ups – 4x6-8 🏋️‍♂️ (strict form)
  • Bodyweight Rows (Table/TRX) – 3x10-12 🔄
  • Archer Rows – 3x6/side 🎯 (scaled: feet on ground)
  • Hanging Leg Raises – 3x12-15 🦵
  • Plank to Failure – 3xMAX ⏳
  • (Stretch: 5 min lats & biceps 🧘‍♂️)
